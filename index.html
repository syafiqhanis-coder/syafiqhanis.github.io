Who We Are

Established in 2017, Foliage has grown into one of Malaysia`s leading producers of premium Microgreens and Edible Flowers. Trusted by more than 1000 culinary outlets nationwide, we supply to top chefs and top tier F&B brands with consistently fresh, high-quality produce harvested daily.

Our focus is simple: sustainable farming, reliable supply, and premium standards. As we expand into international markets, we continue to strengthen partnerships with top culinary establishments while delivering farm-fresh products that meet global expectations.
